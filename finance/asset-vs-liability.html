Asset vs Liability

TL;DR: Assets put money in your pocket. Liabilities take it out.

  • Savings, investments, rental income → assets.
  • Credit cards, car costs, high-interest loans → liabilities.
  • A house with a big mortgage is mostly a liability until debt falls or it earns rent.
